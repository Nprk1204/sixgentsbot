How It Works

1. Join the Queue

Use the /queue command in your Discord server to join the 6 gents queue. Once 6 players have joined, a match will be created.

2. Play the Match

Teams will be formed through a vote system to either be random teams or a captain picks. Play the match with your assigned teammates.

3. Report & Track

Report match results using the /report command. Win to gain MMR and climb the leaderboard!

Discord Bot Features

Queue & Matchmaking

  • Join the queue with a simple command and get matched with other players
  • Choose between random teams or captains selection through voting
  • Climb the leaderboard by winning matches and earning MMR points

Stats & Management

  • Keep track of all your matches and performance over time
  • Get Discord roles based on your in-game Rocket League rank
  • Useful commands for server admins to manage the queue and matches

Command Reference

Command Description
/queue Join the 6 gents queue
/leave Leave the queue
/status Check the current queue status
/report <match_id> <win|loss> Report match results
/leaderboard Get a link to the leaderboard website
/rank [member] Check your rank or another member's rank
